¿Qué hiciste el fin de semana?

What did you get up to this weekend?

Speak your answerTap the circle — about three sentences is plenty.
“El sábado fui a la montaña con mis amigos y hicimos un asado. Estuvo muy divertido pero yo estoy muy cansado ahora.”

A note from your tutorLovely — that really painted a picture. A few small touches and you'd pass for a local:

  1. Lean on “todavía”

    You used the present to say how you feel now — exactly right. Natives often add todavía (“still”) to make the lingering tiredness feel natural.

  2. A more local word

    Asado is perfect in Argentina. Across much of Spain you'll hear barbacoa for the same weekend grill.

  3. The native shortcut

    Instead of estuvo muy divertido, locals reach for a set phrase that just sounds effortless.
